About Yash Computer

Established in 2010, Yash Computer has grown from a local diagnostics repair desk to Chhattisgarh’s premier tech supply and hardware maintenance ecosystem. Over 15 years, we have raised the standards of technology distribution.

Our motto is: "Genuine Spares, Expert Diagnostics, Fair Pricing." We deal with all top brands, offering laptop repair, chip-level logic board recovery, custom workstation construction, and enterprise network setups.
